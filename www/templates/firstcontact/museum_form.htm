help on completing this survey
Important – please read this first

The information you supply here is used to create the searchable online listing for your museum (or other heritage organisation) on museums4schools.

Filling in the basic facts about your museum and its services should be straightforward. The survey also includes questions about the areas of the school curriculum that your museum can support. This could be through:

• permanent displays
• talks and tours
• workshops
• education events
• object loan services
• education outreach services

Teachers can search the website by key stage, subject, and topic – for example “Key Stage 3 History: local Victorian middle class life”. They will want to know if there is plenty for their pupils to see and do, so please only tick topics for which your education provision really could be useful.

The best way to see how your information looks from a teacher’s point of view is to try doing some searches yourself at museums4schools.org

To keep updating to a minimum, the website does not include rapidly changing information. Teachers are encouraged to visit your museum or service’s own website, or ring up to get up-to-date information on temporary exhibitions, events, charging details, and so on.

You don’t need to complete the whole survey in one go. You can click “save” at any time and then come back later. Your information will not appear on the website until you have finished the whole survey and it has been checked through by our editorial staff. After that, you can still change your information anytime by clicking “update” on the museums4schools website.

If you have any questions or problems completing the survey, please email us.
